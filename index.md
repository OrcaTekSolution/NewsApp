Privacy Policy for ETN News

Last updated: 2026

ETN News ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ETN News mobile application. This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using our app.

By using ETN News, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.


 1. Information We Collect

ETN News does not require users to create an account or provide personal information.

However, the app may collect limited information automatically to improve the service, including:

* Device information (device type, OS version)
* App usage statistics
* Language preference selected in the app
* Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used for push notifications

This information does not personally identify users.



2. Push Notifications

ETN News uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver news notifications.

This requires storing a device notification token so that relevant news alerts can be sent to users. These tokens are used only for delivering notifications and are not shared with third parties.

Users can disable notifications at any time from their device settings.

3. News Content

ETN News aggregates publicly available news from various sources and publishers.

We do not claim ownership of third-party content and always provide proper source attribution where applicable.

If any publisher has concerns regarding content aggregation, they may contact us for removal.

4. Data Usage

The limited information collected is used only for:

* Improving app performance
* Delivering relevant notifications
* Understanding general usage trends

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

ETN News may use trusted third-party services including:

* Google Firebase (for notifications and basic analytics)

These services may collect limited information as governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy
